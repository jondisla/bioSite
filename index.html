START

Daniela Arancibia is a principal for a local elementary school. After graduating as a teacher in 2011, she moved abroad and began teaching kindergarten. After years of dedication and helping young minds grow and learn new skills, she also started teaching in the summer programs. Daniela always wanted to teach under privileged kids. While in school, she always made the comment that she would like to teach in remote poor areas.

Her dedication in reaching out to struggling kids has made her a household name in parents of her students and the community she serves in. Never wavering from her goals, Daniela has acted like a mom, nurse, tutor, friend and motivator for her students. Later on she would given more responsibilities in planning and supervising the fulfillment of activities.Her superior has always stated how she was born to teach and she has truly shown that to be a fact.
