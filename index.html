Daniela Arancibia is a principal for a local elementary school. After graduating as a teacher in 2011, she moved abroad and began teaching kindergarten. After years of dedication and helping young minds grow and learn new skills, she also started teaching in the summer programs. Daniela always wanted to teach under privileged kids. While in school, she always made the comment that she would like to teach in remote poor areas.

Her dedication in reaching out to struggling kids has made her a household name for the community sheserves in. Never wavering from her goals, Daniela has been a mom,nurse, tutor, friend and motivator for her students. Later on she would given more responsibilities in planning and supervising the fulfillment of activities. Her superior has always stated how she was born to teach and she has truly shown that to be a fact.

A lot of parents attend the school she teaches at just because they know that she will do a good job teaching their kids. However, there were some challenges Daniela had to face on the road to where she is now. As a decision maker however, Daniela has managed to inspire her team to achieve better results for her team of teachers.

She knows that in order to make other improve, she has to teach them what she knows as a teacher and a principal. Now after almost 10 years of being a teacher, she has been a principle for the past 5 and is still reaching young minds.
